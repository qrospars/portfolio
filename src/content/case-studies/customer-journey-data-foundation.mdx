Personalisation starts with recognising the same customer across every touchpoint. I led and managed the data-foundation track of a program for a global jewellery retailer built around that groundwork.

I mapped the customer journey, translated business requirements into signal and data needs, and facilitated refinement workshops with the client and their partners. The program supports a wider personalisation strategy focused on acquisition, efficiency, and retention.
