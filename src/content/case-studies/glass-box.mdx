Client reporting is often scattered across manual, ad-hoc work. Glass Box is an internal product designed to replace that with a single platform where clients can see their own data, roadmaps, and proposals, while recurring reporting is automated.

I contributed to the product and helped scope its pilot. It addresses both a cost problem, reducing reliance on manual reporting, and a relationship problem: clients need proactive context, not just a result at the end of the month.
